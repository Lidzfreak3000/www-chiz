About

  • What Makes It “Intelligent”?

    Software robots don’t just mindlessly reproduce manual activity anymore. They can actually make intelligent decisions just like a human being would, based on dynamic information. Errors and exceptions can be checked along the way. Alternate actions can be chosen when things don't quite work as expected.

  • A New Understanding

    Intelligent automation software actually has an “understanding” of your business processes and their permutations. The software takes that knowledge into account when executing automated business process validation to verify that the right outcome occurred.

  • Real Change

    When changes are made to underlying enterprise applications (like SAP, Oracle, etc.) the automation software can also point out which business processes are going to be impacted by the change - and therefore need to be tested. Intelligent automation can also update its own understanding and description of those business processes by incorporating what’s changed into its database of existing processes.

  • Who We Are

    Scorpion Engineering and Development (Scorpion) is a technology and data management innovation company headquartered in Orlando, FL. We specialize in providing advanced technology solutions and services to federal agencies, state agencies, and corporations throughout the United States. Leveraging intelligent automation, a multi-disciplinary collaborative environment, and its substantial intellectual property portfolio, Scorpion excels in developing concepts into market-focused products and customer-driven solutions.

  • Distributed Architectures and Solutions

    Scorpion’s solutions provide robust high-performance infrastructures along with services and tools for the rapid development and deployment of large-scale distributed systems. Scorpion has expertise in service-oriented agent frameworks, cognitive systems, semantic service-oriented architecture, and web services. Scorpion designs solutions compatible with distributed interoperability standards, including standards from N.A.M.E and O.W.A.S.P.

  • Human System Integration

    Scorpion uses Human Factors engineering methods to design easy-to-use interfaces that improve human-system functionality, productivity and situational awareness. Scorpion applies current best practices to the design integration of human-machine systems. We create new systems to automate previously un-automated capabilities, apply principles to test new designs in novel conditions, and create designs for existing products or those in development. We conduct cognitive analyses to design interfaces, and run usability tests to assess interfaces, tools and performance. We also create or revise computer interfaces that implement designs for specific products.
